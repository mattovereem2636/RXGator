# RXGator
Free prescription drug price aggregator — helping Americans find the lowest price across every pharmacy and discount platform.  One search. Every platform. The real lowest price.

What is RXGator?
RXGator is a free, independent prescription drug price comparison tool that searches eight data sources simultaneously to find the lowest available price for any medication. Unlike GoodRx or SingleCare, which only show their own negotiated prices, RXGator compares across all of them — plus pharmacy programs, government benchmarks, and mail-order options — to show you the actual lowest price available.
One search. Every platform. The real lowest price.
Why does this exist?
No single discount card is cheapest for every drug at every pharmacy. The winning strategy today is to manually check 2–3 platforms per prescription — which nobody has time to do. RXGator eliminates that friction. We believe prescription pricing should be transparent, and no one should overpay for medication because they didn’t know a cheaper option existed 10 minutes away.
What does RXGator search?
Cost Plus Drugs (Mark Cuban’s transparent pricing) • SingleCare (discount card with CVS, Walgreens, Walmart, Kroger, Costco pricing) • Medicaid NADAC (what pharmacies actually pay — the government benchmark) • RxNorm (drug matching and brand-to-generic resolution from the National Library of Medicine) • MedlinePlus (plain-language drug information from NLM) • openFDA (drug safety and NDC codes) • Walmart Rx Program ($4/$9/$15 generics) • Costco Pharmacy (estimated retail pricing).
